Club Team

S.S. Arg

In August of 2008, with just enough time to get a roster in for sectionals, S.S.Arg sailed onto the Portland scene.

Arg was created for those players in Portland without a club team to call home. No practices to show up to, no harassing emails about paying UPA dues and signing waivers, no recurring knee problems or back injuries-- these people considered Summer League to be the end.

We say, no!

There was too much talent to exploit in this tiny city. Too many college grads with flexible schedules, too many parents with no sideline for their kids, too many flicks going unflicked-- if not for Arg.

And the people came.
